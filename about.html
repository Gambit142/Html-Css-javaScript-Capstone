The Gambit's 5-A-SIDE Tournament is a yearly soccer competition that takes place in Lagos, Nigeria. Teams from all over Africa converge to play against each other in a winner takes all football tournament. The winning team goes home with $50,000 and a sponsorship deal worth $20,000.

The Gambit's 5-A-SIDE Tournament is a yearly soccer competition that takes place in Lagos, Nigeria. Teams from all over Africa converge to play against each other in a winner takes all football tournament. The winning team goes home with $50,000 and a sponsorship deal worth $20,000. The will hold in Nigeria within the Yaba-Lekki Axis in Lagos State. The organizers of the event have also secured 6 state of the art stadiums where the matches will be played in.

The Logo of Gambit's 5-A-SIDE Soccer Tournament 2021 was decided through the "Create Logo" competition held from 15.March to 17.April. A committee was set up for the vetting of logos and the curated ones were put up for online voting by the public. Mr Cage Mbaguno from South Africa emerged as the winner.
